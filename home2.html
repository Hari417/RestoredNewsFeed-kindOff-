Elon Musk

the CEO of Tesla Hints at

India Entry in 2021

Electric car maker Tesla Inc's CEO Elon Musk on Friday hinted at the company's entry into India in 2021.

"Thanks for waiting!" he said to another Twitter user on the same thread.



Jeff Bezos Plans To Open a free

preschool in Oct 19


Bezos posted a photo of a new classroom with children's books on shelves and carpets on the floor on Instagram, saying the first Bezos Academy set to open on October 19 is just the beginning.

"This one in Des Moines (Washington) is the first of many free preschools that we'll be opening for underserved children," Bezos said in the post.

Jack Ma’s Ant Group Wins

Approval For Dual Listing That

May Raise $35 Billion

Ant Group has received approval from the Hong Kong Stock Exchange for its IPO(initial public offering), according to media reports, clearing the way for what is expected to be the world’s largest public listing.

(Ant is an affiliate of Alibaba Group, an $826 billion Chinese firm cofounded by Ma).

The Hangzhou-based fintech giant had formally applied in August to sell its shares simultaneously in Hong Kong and Shanghai.


How Google's 'New' Work Model

Could Help Google Employees To Relieve Stress


According to CNBC, “Google is rethinking its long-term work options for employees, as most of them say they don’t want to come back to the office full-time.”

According to a recent survey of Google employees, “sixty-two percent want to return to their offices at some point, but not every day”. For this reason, the company is working on “hybrid” models for future work.
